INIA TEXAS GENE EXPRESSION DATABASE

Welcome to the INIA Texas Gene Expression Database!

IT-GED contains the top statistical results from genomic studies focusing on models of excessive alcohol consumption. IT-GED provides support for comparing and integrating results across gene expression studies and for generating novel hypotheses. IT-GED is sponsored in part by the INIA Neuroimmune Consortium.

If this is your first visit, you may find it helpful to look at our IT-GED Walkthrough.
